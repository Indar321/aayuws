FAQ

Common questions

How often should RO filters be replaced?
Sediment and carbon filters are typically replaced every 6 months, while the RO membrane lasts 12-24 months depending on water quality and usage. Our AMC plans schedule this automatically.
What is TDS?
TDS (Total Dissolved Solids) measures the minerals and salts dissolved in your water, in mg/litre. The ideal drinking range is 50-150 ppm — our technicians test and calibrate your RO's TDS controller to keep it there.
Do you provide same-day service?
Yes, most service requests within Raipur city limits are attended to the same day, often within 30-60 minutes of booking during business hours.
Which RO purifier is best for home use?
It depends on your input water's TDS level and household size. For most Raipur homes we recommend a 7-10L RO+UV+UF system. Book a free water test and we'll recommend the right model.
What is included in AMC plans?
All AMC plans include scheduled servicing visits, filter and membrane inspection, and discounted spare parts. Higher tiers add priority support and free replacements — see the AMC Plans section above for full details.
